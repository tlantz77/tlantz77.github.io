Tim's Tribute Page to Grizzly Bear

Hey all, one of my favorite bands of recent years is Grizzly Bear! An indie-rock band formed in Brooklyn, New York in 2002, their sound has often been described as chamber pop or neo-psychedelic pop. A few of my personal favorite songs include "Ready, Able", "Sleeping Ute" and "Half-Gate." The current band members consist of:

  • Edward Droste (vocals, keyboards, omnichord)
  • Daniel Rossen (vocals, guitar, keyboards)
  • Chris Taylor (bass, backing vocals)
  • Chris Bear (drums, backing vocals)
